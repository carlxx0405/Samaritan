Why Samaritan?

In emergencies, every second matters. Samaritan ensures faster blood donor discovery, real-time hospital availability updates, and instant notifications for nearby donors.

Blood Donation Facts

  • 1 donation can save up to 3 lives.
  • It only takes 8-10 minutes to donate blood.
  • Donating blood is safe, fast, and helps with blood circulation.
  • There is no substitute for human blood – we need donors.

For more education on blood safety and knowledge. Trusted sources like Malawi Blood Transfusion Service are licensed for more information

How It Works

1. Register

Donors and hospitals register with essential details.

2. Match

The system shows nearby donors and live hospital stock in their respective dashboards.

3. Notify & Donate

Hospitals or users send requests, and donors get alerted.
